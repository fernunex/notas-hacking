## Descripción
What is 0x3D (base 16) in decimal (base 10)?
## Solución

Calculo manual:
A=10
B=11
C=12
D=13

3x16¹+13x16⁰ = 48 + 13= 61

Flag:
```
picoCTF{61}
```
